## What counts as an event?

An **event** is any execution of a workflow step. Each time a trigger fires and runs through your workflow steps, each step counts as one event.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does a trigger count as an event?">
    Yes. Each workflow execution counts the trigger step plus every subsequent step that runs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I exceed my monthly limit?">
    On the free tier, workflow executions are paused until your quota resets at the start of the next billing cycle. On paid plans, additional events are billed at a per-event rate. You will receive an email notification before your quota is exhausted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does my event quota reset?">
    Quotas reset on the first day of each calendar month (UTC).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I purchase additional events without upgrading my plan?">
    Yes. On paid plans you can purchase event add-ons from the billing settings page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Upgrading your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open billing settings">
    Navigate to [https://pipedream.com/settings/billing](https://pipedream.com/settings/billing).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Select the plan that fits your usage and click **Upgrade**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment details">
    Pipedream uses Stripe for payment processing. Enter your credit card details and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Changes take effect immediately">
    Your new event quota and features activate as soon as the payment is confirmed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
